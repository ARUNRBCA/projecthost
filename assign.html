WELCOME TO H.H THE RAJAH'S COLLAGE

H.H The Rajah's Collage (Autonomous Accredited B+) is a Government institutions and is affiliated to Bharathidasan university,Tiruchirapalli. The Collage is catering to the needs of economically and socially weaker sections of the society, especially the first of generation of learners of Pudukkottai District. The Collage has based its motto on the vedic dictum "Thamasoma Jyotrigmiya" (Irulil Iruthu oliyai Nokki) Which means "from Darkness" To Light" The goal reflects the objectives of higher education with emphasis on the regional demands.
